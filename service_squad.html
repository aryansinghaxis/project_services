Services related to Cleaner

A cleaner is responsible for maintaining cleanliness and hygiene in spaces by sweeping, mopping, dusting, and sanitizing surfaces. They ensure a tidy environment, often in offices, homes, or public areas, contributing to health and safety. Effective cleaners are detail-oriented, reliable, and use appropriate cleaning tools and supplies.

Services related to Plumber

A plumber is a skilled professional who installs, repairs, and maintains water and drainage systems in homes and buildings. They handle tasks such as fixing leaks, unclogging drains, and installing pipes. Plumbers ensure safe and efficient water supply and sanitation, contributing to overall building functionality

Services related to Carpenters

A carpenter is a skilled tradesperson who works with wood to build, repair, and install structures such as furniture, doors, frameworks, and cabinets. They use tools to cut, shape, and assemble materials. Carpenters play a vital role in construction and home improvement, ensuring both functionality and aesthetic appeal.
